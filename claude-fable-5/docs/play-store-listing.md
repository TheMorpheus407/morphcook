## Full description (EN)

```
recipe apps treat your diet like a filter. you say "vegan" and the döner
disappears. you say "gluten-free" and pasta night is over. morphcook
refuses.

here, the same dish exists for every body. the vegan döner is not a
substitution table — it's a fully written recipe, with its own
ingredients, its own steps, its own pride. gluten-free alfredo, keto
burger, low-fodmap shakshuka: all real recipes, all already in the book.

tell the app how you eat — diets, allergies, single ingredients you'd
rather avoid, your calorie target, the minutes you actually have — and
your cookbook quietly becomes yours. no "adapted for you" banners. no
machinery on display.

what's inside:
· bilingual recipes (english & german), written with care
· per-dish variant switching: diet, effort, calorie level
· a cookbook that saves *your* version of a dish
· weekly meal planning with drag & drop
· a smart shopping list that adds garlic to garlic (2 cloves + 3 cloves
  = 5 cloves) and sorts by aisle
· cook mode: dark, step by step, with timers, a servings scaler and a
  visual flash alert for deaf and hard-of-hearing cooks
· encrypted, file-based backups you fully own

and what's not inside: accounts, ads, tracking, cloud, network calls.
the app is completely offline. your data never leaves your device.
```
