# Corpus expansion brief (wave 3)

Same contract as [corpus-brief.md](corpus-brief.md) — schema, hard rules,
voice. Plus the review-wave lessons, now mandatory:

- **Variant pride**: never frame a variant by absence or imitation. No
  "instead of", "replaces", "fools everyone", "no X, no problem", "where
  the X used to be". Lead with what the dish IS.
- **No copy-paste tropes** across recipes ("nobody will ask…").
- **Every recipe gets salt** (or an explicit salty element) and every
  listed ingredient is used by the steps — including prep (slice/halve).
- **Timer physics**: timer_minutes must match the prose and reality
  (batches need batch-sized timers).
- **German**: idiomatisch, du-Form, keine Anglizismen (kein "pulsieren",
  "pfannenrühren", "häckseln", "durchziehen" für commit), Substantive
  groß, DE-Titel mit großem Anfangsbuchstaben.
- **EN**: lowercase incl. "°c"; no "simply"/"delicious"; no
  "not X. just Y." cadence.

New ingredients this wave: `pork-cutlet, sage, couscous, macaroni,
dumpling-wrappers, dried-apricots, green-curry-paste`.
